Creating an Application

  • Goto Discord Developers Page
  • Click on Create Application button
  • Setup your Application by giving a Name and an Avatar
  • Then, click on Bot in the left sidebar and click on Bot User to create a Bot.
  • Invite your Bot by replacing CLIENTID
https://discordapp.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=CLIENTID&scope=bot&permissions=8
