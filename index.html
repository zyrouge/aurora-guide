Why should you not host your own instance?

If you new to programming and doesn't know NodeJS, you must not try to host one. You are free to host you own instance for your specific guild.

Requirements

  • NodeJS v12 or greater installed
  • Basic knowledge of Nodejs
  • A good place to host
